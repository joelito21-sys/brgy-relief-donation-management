## Slide 2: Project Overview & Objectives
**Goal:** To create a centralized, digital platform that connects residents in need with donors and administrators during calamities.
- **Efficiency:** Replace manual lists with digital records.
- **Transparency:** Track donations from receipt to distribution.
- **Accessibility:** Easy-to-use portals for Residents and Donors.
- **Speed:** Faster response times for relief requests.

---

## Slide 3: Key User Roles
The system accommodates three distinct user types:

1.  **Residents**: The beneficiaries who request assistance.
2.  **Donors**: Individuals or entities providing financial/material aid.
3.  **Administrators**: Barangay officials managing the operations.

---

## Slide 4: Resident Portal Features
**Empowering the Community**
- **Secure Registration:** Detailed profile creation with address validation (Purok/Sitio selection).
- **Relief Requests:** Easy submission of needs (Food, Medical, Cash).
    - *New:* Specific "Cash Assistance" selection (₱1,000 - ₱10,000).
- **Status Tracking:** Real-time updates on request status (Pending, Approved, Distributed).
- **Security:** Email verification and OTP-based Password Reset.

---

## Slide 5: Donor Portal Features
**Facilitating Generosity**
- **Flexible Donations:** Support for various donation types:
    - **Cash:** Integrated flow for GCash, PayMaya, and Bank Transfers.
    - **In-Kind:** Food, Clothing, Medicine, and Walk-in appointments.
- **Dashboard:** View donation history and impact summaries.
- **Transparency:** Digital receipts and "Thank You" acknowledgments.
- **Security:** Secure login and OTP Password Recovery.

---

## Slide 6: Admin Dashboard (The Command Center)
**Operational Control**
- **Analytics:** Real-time stats on total internees, donations, and pending requests.
- **Request Management:** Review, approve, or reject relief requests with a click.
- **Inventory System:** Track stock levels of relief goods (Rice, Canned Goods, etc.).
- **Distribution Management:** Organizes scheduled relief distributions to approved residents.

---

## Slide 7: Unique Innovations
- **Hybrid Address System:** Smart selection for Puroks/Sitios with generic "Other" text input for precision.
- **Automated Communication:** 
    - Email notifications for new requests (to Admin).
    - OTP emails for password security.
- **Walk-in Management:** Dedicated workflow for scheduling in-person donations.

---

## Slide 8: Technology Stack
Built for reliability and scale:
- **Backend:** PHP / Laravel Framework (Robust & Secure).
- **Database:** MySQL (Structured data management).
- **Frontend:** Blade Templates with Tailwind CSS (Modern, mobile-responsive design).
- **Authentication:** Custom Secure Auth with multi-guard support (Resident/Donor/Admin).

---

## Slide 9: Impact & Benefits
- **Zero Redundancy:** Prevents double-claiming of relief goods.
- **Accountability:** Every peso and relief pack is accounted for.
- **Speed:** Drastically reduces the time between "Request" and "Relief".
- **Data-Driven:** Helps officials plan better for future calamities based on data.
